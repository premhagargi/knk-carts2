# **App Name**: KnK Karts: Unleashed

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Hero & Dynamic Navigation: An immersive, motion-driven hero section with massive typography, racing visuals, GSAP animations, particle streaks, and dynamic background color transitions (black to deep red to electric blue). The navigation morphs from transparent to blurred glass on scroll.
- Horizontal Product Showcase: Three cinematic product sections (Recreational Karts, Off-Road Buggies, Race Karts) featuring horizontal scroll, large image parallax, and scroll-triggered color theme changes for an editorial luxury feel.
- Interactive Track Ecosystem Grid: An experimental border grid detailing KnK's complete turnkey karting ecosystem, showcasing elements like Track Design and Fleet Management, with interactive hover effects including lift, glowing borders, and subtle motion.
- Global Legacy & Blueprint Visuals: Editorial layout for the '25 Years' section, combined with a dark, technical blueprint theme featuring moving lines, CAD overlays, construction visuals, and large editorial typography.
- Global Trust Marquee & Engagement Forms: An auto-scrolling marquee displaying KnK Karts' global presence and testimonial placeholders, integrated with minimalist newsletter subscription and contact forms for inquiries.
- Advanced Motion & Performance Optimization: Implementation of Lenis smooth scroll (lerp: 0.1, duration: 1.2, smoothTouch/Wheel true), Framer Motion for scroll reveals, parallax effects, background color interpolation with silky transitions, Next.js Image optimization, and responsive design principles.
- AI-Powered Support Chatbot: A clickable Genkit-powered AI tool widget designed to answer user questions based on the content of the entire website.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark color scheme with a primary vibrant electric blue (#128EFF) for highlights and interactive elements, conveying futuristic motorsport energy. The background will be a very dark desaturated bluish-grey (#101417), aligning with the overall cool palette.
- Accent color will be a vivid red (#FC2121), strategically used for call-to-action buttons and to emphasize 'motorsport energy' and brand contrast.
- Dynamic background color transitions: the page background will smoothly interpolate between black, deep red, and electric blue during scroll for an immersive experience.
- Primary font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for all text. Typography will feature massive headings, tight tracking, uppercase hero titles, minimal body text, and small caps for meta-information, strictly adhering to a modern, sharp, and bold aesthetic. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use sharp, geometric, and minimalist vector icons. Avoid rounded iconography; focus on elements that reflect precision, speed, and advanced engineering, consistent with the futuristic aesthetic.
- Zero border radius anywhere, emphasizing sharp edges for all elements including buttons, cards, and images. Layouts will feature editorial principles, dramatic whitespace, split sections, experimental border grids, and maintain a high-end Webflow/Framer template style.
- Extensive motion design utilizing Lenis for smooth scrolling (lerp: 0.1, duration: 1.2s), GSAP for complex hero animations, and Framer Motion for scroll reveal fade, parallax, horizontal scroll zones, and background color interpolation, all with silky 0.8–1.2s transitions.
